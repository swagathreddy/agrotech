About Agritourism

Agritourism is a growing trend that offers farmers the opportunity to diversify their income and connect with consumers. It involves opening up farms to visitors and offering educational and recreational activities. By participating in agritourism, farmers can increase their visibility, build relationships with customers, and promote agricultural education. From farm tours and tastings to workshops and farm stays, agritourism offers something for everyone.
